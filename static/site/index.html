Case Studies

Data-Driven Decision Making

Problem Domain: As a cloud-enabled e-commerce retailer, our client had services in place for customer sentiment analysis and predictive analytics. However, there was no real-time services that mapped organizational KPI's and market velocity. A better solution was needed to measure the performance of sales and customer need.

Biblio Solution: Development of a data-driven decision making system that that utilized input KPI's to train a prediction model and measure churn and buyer intent.

Result: a 3% increase in market capitalization within the first 6 months.

SAAS (MULTI-TENANT)

Problem Domain: A legacy ecosystem of a health-service provider was composed of several on-premise clustered databases shared across multiple applications. The system was difficult to manage, scale and update. As a result our client remained locked into a single base offering of capabilities fixed to a set of service features that continously lost compliance certifications over time.

Biblio Solution: Re-achitecture to a cloud-based multi-tenant solution that improved reliability, sustainability and personalization on a per-customer basis. Implement and certify HIPAA complancy.

Result: lowered annual operational costs by as much as 40%, and improved monitoring of compliancy by 80%.

CLOUD MIGRATION

Problem Domain: Although our client had optimized its service-oriented systems in the past, it had not yet considered how cloud-based platforms could improve service channel redundancy and scalability.

Biblio Solution: a road map that included the selection of a cloud vendor, a strategic plan and cost-benefit analysis detailing lift-and-shift, refactoring and big-bang approaches to application migration.

Result: a 30% reduction in sustainability costs and 20% scale-up of visibility into system availability.
